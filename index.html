Here's a little page about my love of festivals, and specifically North Coast©, my most memorable one yet...

Festival life

Nothing quite compares to spending 3 days with your favorite people while listening to your favorite music in an adult playground. The stages, the lights, the lasers, the graphics, the bass...all coming together for the most immersive musical experience you can find. But it's more than that...everyone b eing 100% their most authentic self, dressed in whatever their heart desires, practicing radical exceptance and spreading love and light, united in their love of the music.

Let's talk costumes

The scene

North Coast really put together something special this year. Their stages were placed perfectly, allowing so much room for everyone to spread out, dance, vibe, while limiting any carryover sound from surrounding stages. That's hard to do, considering there were 3 artists playing at a time at each of the main stages, plus a few smaller stages hosting various lesser known artists. The sound systems were top notch, the stage effects, the lasers and lights, and the nightime fireworks were phenomenal. And all the fun props, art exhibits and interactive spaces made for a lot of fun exploring.

All about the music

The real reason to go, of course, is the music. EDM has been a favorite of mine since as long as I can remember. The music speaks to my soul, moves my body, and has facilitated connections with some of the best people I've ever met. I got to check a couple big names off my bucket list this year: Zeds Dead, Ganja White Knight, Alison Wonderland, ATLiens, and Big Gigantic. Got to see some of my favorites as well, like Liquid Stranger and Black Tiger Sex Machine. Each day of the festival had a lineup that left nothing to be desired. I danced till my knees hurt and smiled till my face hurt. Shyness gets checked at the door when it comes to your dance moves. You move the way the music makes you feel and no one is there judging what that looks like.

Being able to enjoy this festival with my partner (it was our first together) and our best friends made this weekend in Chicago a truly magical experience. It is a beautiful thing to share that time with people who love you and who share the same love of this music and this life with you. I hope everyone get a chance to experience this at least once. It's lifechanging.
